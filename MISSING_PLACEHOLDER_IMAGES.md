## Image Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Format:** PNG with transparency or white background
- **Dimensions:** 1024x1024px (square)
- **File Size:** < 100KB (optimized for web)
- **Style:** Consistent with existing placeholders
- **Lighting:** Professional food photography style
- **Background:** Clean, minimal, food-focused

### Naming Convention
- Format: `{category}-{cut}.png`
- Lowercase, hyphen-separated
- Examples: `res-ribeye.png`, `cordero-chuleta.png`, `pato-magret.png`

### Photography Tips
1. **Angle:** 45¬∞ angle or straight-on shot
2. **Focus:** Sharp focus on the meat
3. **Color:** Accurate meat color (not oversaturated)
4. **Garnish:** Minimal or none (focus on the cut)
5. **Plate:** Simple white or wooden board
